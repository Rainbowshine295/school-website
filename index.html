Hello!

This is the website for Vidya Vikasini English High School. Our school is spread across a wide area of 35 acres. With a dedicated staff and ever-enthusiastic students, our campus is always full of hustle and bustle. Gifted with amazing flora and fauna, one feels a sense of greenery when in the campus.

What all parents want is their child getting good education. Our staff will help you achieve that. Do you care about pollution? No worries. There is no air pollution because of the variety of trees in the campus. No sound pollution, except the noise of children.
